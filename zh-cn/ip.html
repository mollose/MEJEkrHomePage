Services

我們的服务

01

Worldbuilding Library Creation

We analyze the essence of your IP and build a comprehensive Setting Bible. From terminology wikis, timelines, and spatial structures to narrative architecture — we systematically design the knowledge assets that serve as the foundation for IP expansion.

Setting Bible IP Wiki Timeline 世界观架构
02

角色人設 & 设计

We create original characters that reflect your IP's identity. Deliverables include persona settings, visual designs, and character sheets (design guides), with the option to choose between character licensing or full transfer of rights.

角色設定表 Persona 设计 Visual Guide 授权/转讓
03

粉丝活动 Platform 开发

We build interactive fandom platforms powered by your characters. Daily stories, character care, mini-games, digital goods, photo cards — we create a content ecosystem that fans engage with every day. Supporting 40+ languages, we reach global fandoms without separate overseas promotions.

Web Platform 每日內容 数字商品 40+種语言

IP扩展与衍生业务

Character merchandise, digital goods, limited-edition physical goods, crowdfunding tie-ins — we plan and operate IP-derived secondary businesses driven by data. Fan preference analysis enables precise product planning.
