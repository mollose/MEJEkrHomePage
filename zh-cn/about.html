使命

使命

我們的使命

"任何人都不应该感到孤独"

MEJE Works builds worldview-driven communities that connect and comfort people in the modern age. Drawing on 30 years of experience designing how humans form bonds and find solace online — from the BBS era to the metaverse — we are creating a world where entertainment transcends mere amusement and becomes a social-emotional safety net. A small act of caring for an idol character can restore the rhythm of a fractured daily life, and communication born within a fandom can expand into broader social connection — we dissolve isolation through the power of metaphorical worlds.
